// What is BYOK?

Your own API key – full control, zero overhead.

BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) means you connect Aura directly to an AI provider of your choice using your own API key. You only pay for what you actually use – without consuming your monthly Aura messages.

BYOK is an optional alternative in Aura: on the Free plan you get 30 free messages per day. If you need more, or want specific models, simply activate your own key.

💡 Simply put: BYOK is like using your own SIM card in someone else's phone – you use the app, but pay the provider directly.
